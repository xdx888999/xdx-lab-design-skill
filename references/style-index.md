# Design Style Index
# 50 个精选风格的精简索引 — 供 Skill 快速选择使用

> 每个条目：风格名、核心色、关键词、最佳使用场景

## Batch 1 — Web/UI 核心风格

**01 · Glassmorphism Dark** `#0D0D1A` + `#8B5CF6` (purple)
Keywords: glass, blur, transparent, dark, modern, cards
Best for: AI products, creative SaaS, music apps, portfolio, anything that needs "wow factor" at night

**02 · Tech Minimal** `#FFFFFF` + `#0A0A0A` (near-black)
Keywords: clean, minimal, white, precise, Inter, Vercel-style
Best for: Dev tools, technical products, startup homepage, any product where "less is more"

**03 · Aurora UI** `#06061A` + `#818CF8` (indigo)
Keywords: aurora, gradient blobs, cosmic, glowing, Anthropic/Perplexity style
Best for: AI companies, next-gen tech, products targeting forward-thinking audiences

**04 · Neobrutalism** `#FFFEF5` + `#FFDD00` (brutal yellow)
Keywords: brutal, chunky, bold, offset-shadow, no-radius, Gumroad-style
Best for: Creative tools, indie products, portfolios, Gen-Z brands, anything that should feel different

**05 · FinTech Clean** `#FFFFFF` + `#6366F1` (Stripe purple)
Keywords: financial, trust, clean, data, Stripe/Wise/Mercury style
Best for: Payment products, banking apps, fintech startups, anything handling money
