Our Practice

The beauty of the unfinished.

We have been making ceramics in the same Kyoto workshop since 1971. Our methods have not changed. We do not use electric wheels. We fire only in wood-burning kilns, accepting that the flame makes decisions we cannot.

Wabi-sabi is not a style choice. It is a way of seeing — finding the beautiful in what is impermanent, incomplete, imperfect. A crack in the glaze is not a flaw. It is the object's story, told honestly.
