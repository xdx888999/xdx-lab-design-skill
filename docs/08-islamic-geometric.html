Where the ancient art of the Islamic world finds its contemporary voice

Al Noor is a cultural institute dedicated to the study, preservation, and celebration of Islamic art, architecture, and intellectual heritage.

Our Programs

Three pillars of cultural excellence

١

Living Exhibitions

Curated displays of Islamic geometric art, calligraphy, and architecture from the 8th century to today — presented in their full historical context.

٢

Academic Research

A fellowship program supporting scholars working at the intersection of Islamic civilization, contemporary art, and cross-cultural dialogue.

٣

Craft Workshops

Hands-on sessions in geometric pattern design, Kufic calligraphy, and traditional tilework — guided by master artisans from Morocco and Turkey.
