The Workshop

The hand
teaches the
eye.

Bauhaus was not a school of theory. It was a school of making. Every student spends half their time in the workshop — woodworking, metalworking, weaving, ceramics, printing. The discipline of the material teaches what no lecture can.

Our workshops are open every day from 7am. There are no scheduled hours — only the constraint that the work must be finished.
